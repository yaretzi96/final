Lucky

This page is about my dog, Lucky. She is a 2 year old Female Golden Retriever. She is a very lovable dog, and always on the go for adventures. This is her timeline. which shows a few pictutes, from when she was a puppy, to the the adult dog that she is now.

Personality

Lucky's personality is very hyper and excited about everything. She is a very friendly dog, she loves people especially kids. She is very sweet and she gets distracted easily over things. For instance she loves squirrels ( like any dog) but mostly birds. She loves to be outside in the yard and look up at the birds fly in the sky, that also includes airplanes. Her favorite toys are the squeeker toys, if it does not have one its no good.
